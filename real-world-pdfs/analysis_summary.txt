================================================================================

Traditional parsers fail on these PDFs because they:

1. IGNORE SPATIAL POSITIONING:
   - Cannot handle multi-column layouts (text extracted in wrong order)
   - Fail to recognize table structures without explicit markup
   - Mix up headers, footers, and body text

2. LACK STRUCTURE AWARENESS:
   - Cannot distinguish between background graphics and content
   - Fail to group related text blocks
   - Cannot infer semantic meaning from layout

3. POOR HANDLING OF MS OFFICE ARTIFACTS:
   - MS Office PDFs have quirky spacing and positioning
   - Text may be split into many small fragments
   - Font embedding issues common

4. TEXT EXTRACTION ISSUES:
   - Encoding problems with special characters
   - Excessive whitespace from poor layout understanding
   - Missing text from image-based content

OUR SPATIAL-AWARE APPROACH ADDRESSES THESE BY:

1. Analyzing X/Y coordinates of all text elements
2. Detecting columns, tables, and structured regions
3. Maintaining reading order based on spatial layout
4. Separating background elements from content
5. Using font and position to infer document structure
